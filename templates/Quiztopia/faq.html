FAQ Page

  1. What is Quiztopia?

    Quiztopia is an interactive quiz platform where users can test their knowledge, create their own quizzes, and compete with others on the leaderboard.

  2. Do I need to create an account to take quizzes?

    No, unregistered users can take quizzes without creating an account as well as rate them. However, registering provides additional features like quiz creation and the leaderboard.

  3. How can I register for an account?

    You can register by clicking on the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage and filling out the required fields. It only takes a few seconds!

  4. Can I create my own quizzes?

    Absolutely! Registered users can create and customize their own quizzes.

  5. How does the leaderboard work?

    The leaderboard tracks and displays the top users based on their quiz scores and participation. Compete with others to see who ranks at the top!

  6. Can I rate quizzes?

    Yes! After completing a quiz, registered users have the option to rate it, giving feedback to the creators and helping other users find great content.

  7. What should I do if I encounter a problem or have feedback?

    We welcome your feedback! You can reach out to us through our contact page or send an email to 3070943s@student.gla.ac.uk.

  8. Is there a mobile app available?

    Currently, we only have a web application. However, we are considering developing a mobile app in the future based on user demand.
